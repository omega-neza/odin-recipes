Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Enjoy the irresistible taste of homemade peanut butter cookies. These soft and chewy treats feature the perfect blend of creamy peanut butter, delivering a comforting and delightful experience. Share them with loved ones or savor them alongside a glass of milk for a simple yet satisfying snack.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup shortening
  • ½ cup crunchy peanut butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more as needed
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Cream shortening, peanut butter, and both sugars in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  2. Beat in the egg until incorporated.
  3. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl; gradually stir into peanut butter mixture until combined.
  4. Cover and refrigerate the dough for at least 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. Roll chilled dough into walnut-sized balls and place them 2 inches apart onto baking sheets.
  7. Flatten dough balls with a fork dipped in flour to form a crosswise pattern.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the edges are set, 10 to 12 minutes.
  9. Transfer cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
